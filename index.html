Every Feature

One platform. Every tool
a photographer needs.

AI Neural Sort
Proprietary neural network trained on 50M+ professional photos. Evaluates sharpness, exposure, composition, and emotional impact simultaneously.
Smart Categorization
Automatically tags and groups photos by scene type — ceremony, reception, portraits, details — so your workflow starts organized from the moment upload ends.
Duplicate Removal
Perceptual hash matching finds visually identical frames across burst sequences, auto-selects the sharpest version and archives the rest — saving hours of manual culling.
Face Clustering
Recognizes and groups photos by subject across an entire shoot. Instantly pull every photo of the bride, the groom, or a specific family — no manual searching.
Color Correction Preview
AI suggests tone and white-balance adjustments based on your existing edit history. Preview corrections across your whole gallery before applying any changes.
Export Pipeline
Batch export with custom presets for web, print, and social. Watermarking, metadata stripping, file renaming, and folder structure — all automated and customizable.
Branded Client Galleries
Stunning password-protected galleries with your logo, custom domain, and color palette. Clients browse, favorite, and download their selections without needing an account.
One-Click Delivery
Share a gallery link via email or SMS the moment you're done editing. No ZIP files, no Dropbox, no friction — clients get a native gallery experience instantly.
Client Comments & Selections
Clients mark favorites, leave photo-level comments, and request prints directly in the gallery. You see all feedback in one organized inbox without checking your email.
Expiry & Access Controls
Set gallery expiration dates, download limits, and password requirements. Enforce your usage rights without relying on client goodwill — it's all automated.
Print Store Integration
Let clients order prints, albums, and wall art directly from their gallery. You earn a markup on every order — zero fulfilment work required on your end.
Gallery Analytics
See exactly which photos clients viewed most, how long they spent in the gallery, and which images converted to downloads or print orders — data that sharpens your editing eye.
CRM & Client Portal
Track every client relationship from first inquiry to final delivery. See contract status, payment history, and upcoming shoots in a single timeline view per client.
Invoicing & Payments
Send professional invoices with Stripe-powered payment links. Auto-reminders for late payments, retainer collection, and deposit schedules — all built in.
Smart Contracts
Legally binding digital contracts with e-signature. Use our photographer-specific templates or upload your own. Clients sign on any device in under 60 seconds.
Booking & Scheduling
Embed a booking page on your website. Sync with Google Calendar, collect questionnaires, and automatically block availability. No more back-and-forth emails.
Automated Client Comms
Pre-built email sequences for booking confirmation, shoot reminders, gallery ready notifications, and review requests. All personalized, all on autopilot.
Revenue Reporting
Monthly income breakdowns by shoot type, client, and revenue source. Know your most profitable niches and peak seasons so you can market and price strategically.
Native iOS & Android App
Import directly from your camera roll, review AI sorts on the go, and approve galleries from anywhere — your full workflow in your pocket, with offline support.
Shoot-Day Tethering
Connect your mirrorless or DSLR via tethering. Photos land in View1 Sort the moment you press the shutter — AI starts sorting while you're still shooting the reception.
Heat Map Analytics
Visualize client engagement across your gallery. See which compositions clients linger on, which they skip, and use the data to refine your shooting style over time.
Lightroom & Capture One Sync
Two-way plugin sync with Lightroom Classic and Capture One. View1 Sort's AI selections and categories become smart collections — no duplicate effort.
Team Collaboration
Invite second shooters, retouchers, and studio managers with role-based permissions. Comment on photos, assign tasks, and track progress — all inside View1 Sort.
Backup & Cloud Storage
Every photo backed up to geo-redundant cloud storage the moment it uploads. Versioned history lets you restore any edit state up to 90 days back.
